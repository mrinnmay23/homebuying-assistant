Key terms in simple

What is a mortgage?

A long-term loan used to buy a home. You pay it back monthly, with interest.

Down payment

The cash you pay up front (e.g., 10% of price). A higher down payment can lower your monthly payment.

Interest rate

The cost of borrowing, shown as a yearly %. Even small changes matter a lot over 30 years.

Fees (a.k.a. closing costs)

Extra costs charged by lenders or services (processing, origination, points). Our tools let you compare rate AND fees.
